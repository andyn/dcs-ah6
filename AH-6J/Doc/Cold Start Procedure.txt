1. Power Selector Switch up for battery (B) or down for ground power (LCTRL+B) (ground crew must connect ground power for this option)
2. Hold Starter Switch (Home) until N1 RPM is above 13%
3. Push Thottle Idle cutoff (End) to allow fuel flow to start and continue holding Starter Switch until N1 RPM reaches 58+%
4. Turn on Generator and Inverter Switches
5. Increase N1 RPM to 100% by turning throttle (Page Up)


Rearming is only possible with engineoff


N: Toggle Night Vision Goggles
LALT+L: Flashlight
1: Pilot Seat
2: Copilot Seat
